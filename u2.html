SYNOPSIS

In this sequel, Poppy and Branch discover six other Troll tribes over six different lands. Each one of them is devoted to a different form of music, including Pop, Funk, Classical, Techno, Country and Rock. But Queen Barb is determined to destroy all other kinds of music in favour of Rock. Poppy and Branch set out to unite all the trolls in the hope of stopping her. But can they do it in time?
